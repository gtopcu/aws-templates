adb logcat > logfile.txx
adb logcat -t 100 (-d) -f logfile.txx
adb logcat tail -n 100 > log.txt

V    Verbose (default for <tag>)
D    Debug (default for '*')
I    Info
W    Warn
E    Error
F    Fatal
S    Silent (suppress all output)
